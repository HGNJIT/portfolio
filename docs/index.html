About Me

Current Studies:

I am currently fully employed as a Business Analyst for a large insurance company. In this role I have gained a deep appreciation for programming. My background is in computer hardware and I hold many degrees and certificates in the field. Computer Hardware has always been a fascinating field for me but since I started working as a BA, I have gained a new appreciation for the Software side of things. With that in mind, I decided to attend NJIT to pursue a new understanding of computer software.

Future Plans:

As I gain more knowledge in the field of Information Systems, I wish to start applying them in real world settings. After obtaining my degree from NJIT, I hope to find work in the Information Systems field. I know that it means starting a new career at the bottom of the totem pole, but I am exited and willing to learn new skills at a company that will match my drive for success.
